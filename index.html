About Ethan Rotolo

Meet Ethan, a web developer and much more

Ethan Rotolo is a kindhearted and hardworking individual of many hobbies and skill sets. He is fascinated in music and specializes in both acoustic and electric guitar. His favorite genres all revolve around guitar as well, such as fast heavy metal, surf rock, other guitar oriented genres. He is also very artistic in illustration and creates sketches and logos as a pastime. Ethan is also dedicated to fitness and martial arts, and has earned his second degree black belt in Taekwondo. He still practices consistently to this day. Ethan has also been fulfilling his lifelong dream of surfing for a few years, and has excelled in shredding the waves.
